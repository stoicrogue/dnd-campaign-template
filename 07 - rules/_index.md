# Rules

Homebrew mechanics, house rules, and rule clarifications. Keep this folder focused on rules that deviate from or extend the base game.

## What Goes Here

- **Homebrew mechanics** — custom subsystems (crafting, downtime, reputation, etc.)
- **House rules** — modifications to standard D&D 5e rules at your table
- **Minion rules** — MCDM-style or custom minion stat block conventions
- **Reference tables** — conditions, action economy reminders, concentration tracking

## Rule Document Format

```markdown
# [Rule Name]

## Overview

*What is this rule for? Why does it exist?*

## Mechanics

*Clear, step-by-step explanation of how it works.*

## Examples

*2-3 concrete examples to clarify edge cases.*

## Designer Notes

*Why this approach? What problems does it solve?*
```

## Tips for Homebrew Rules

- Keep rules **short and memorable** — if it takes 5 minutes to explain at the table, simplify it
- Playtest before writing the final version — mechanics often change during actual play
- Note the **session number** when a rule was introduced or changed
